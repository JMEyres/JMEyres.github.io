Hey! I'm Josh, a games programmer and creative technologist. I recently graduated with a Master's in Human-Centred AI for Games Development, following an undergraduate degree in Games Software Engineering. I sit right at the intersection of low-level engine architecture, graphics programming, and modern AI integration.


To give you the quick specs: I finished my Master's with a Distinction and my Bachelor's with a 2:1. During my undergrad, I spent an extended 16-month placement engineering simulations at Drilling Systems. I'm also a massive advocate for game jams—having competed in 7 of them, where my teams secured a 3rd place finish and twice won "Best Mechanic." When I'm not deep in a C++ codebase or profiling a render graph, you'll usually find me out mountain biking, practicing photography, video editing, or diving into whatever game takes my fancy.
