This project was my assignment for my Games Design Principles unit, for my games software engineering course at bournemouth university. The brief for this project was to get into a group of four and make either a card game or a board game. We chose to make a card game as we felt it would be more interesting. For this game we had to design a game of our choosing, regularly meet and record any progress that has been made. We then also had to make a ten minute video presenting our game. Within this video we had to explain why our game delivers meaningful play, Describe the design in terms of a formal framework (FFE or MDA), and then two or maybe more of: 

1.  Mechanical design with regards to space, time, resources and economics
2.  Use of probability and uncertainty
3.  Puzzle design
4.  Information design
5.  Elegant rule design and table top best practice
6.  Considerations for game difficulty and complexity
7.  Game balance
